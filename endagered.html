Aristolochia merxmuelleri


The steno-endemic species Aristolochia merxmuelleri Greuter et E. Mayer is the species of serpentine, which grows in Kosovo in a very limited area. It can be found at the foot of Koznik Mountain between the localities of Llapçevë and Mrasor, on the left side of the river Mirusha, in bushy places with a rocky serpentine base at altitudes of 400–660 m.
