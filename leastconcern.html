Gentiana punctata L


Gentiana punctata, the spotted gentian, is a least concern herbaceous species of flowering plant in the Gentian family Gentianaceae. It grows in Central and Southeastern Europe at altitudes 1.500−2.600 meters. It is 20−60 cm tall.

Lilium albanicum


Perennial, up to 60 cm tall bulbous plant. Bulb of medium size, reddish. Tree erect, slender, bare and leafless in the lower part and upwards with alternately arranged, mutually compacted, linearly lanceolate leaves.
